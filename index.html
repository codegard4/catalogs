GSC 240 Sky Map

Click here for more information about the GSC240 Catalog.
Click here for the GSC240 MySQL database description & schema
  • The Guide Star Catalog (GSC) supports the operational needs of the Hubble Space Telescope and contains nearly 19 million objects brighter than sixteenth magnitude, with over 15 million classified as stars.
  • The GSC provides positions and magnitudes for the stars, and various versions of the catalog are available on the STScI Website.
  • Guide Star Catalog Version 1.2 includes astrometric recalibration and refinements compared to Version 1.1, with an estimated overall rms error better than 0.3 arcseconds.
  • The GSC 1.2 reduction references the "Positions and Proper Motions Catalogue" (PPM) and the Astrographic Catalogue (AC) for removing mean systematics from plates.
  • The HEASARC has not combined objects with multiple listings in the GSC 1.2 Catalog, and the recommended browse cone search radius is 30 arcminutes, with a default radius of 1 arcminute. GSC 1.2 is not recommended for HST phase 2 coordinates, as it has not been installed in the HST ground system.
